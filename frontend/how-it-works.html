How It Works

Fund My Startup platform follows a simple and structured process to connect startups and investors efficiently.

Step 1: Registration – Users register as either Startup or Investor by providing basic details.

Step 2: Profile Creation – Startups create detailed profiles including business idea, domain, and funding requirements.

Step 3: Exploration – Investors browse and explore startups based on their interests and investment capacity.

Step 4: Connection – Investors can connect with startups and show interest in funding.

Step 5: Growth – Startups receive funding and grow their business with investor support.
